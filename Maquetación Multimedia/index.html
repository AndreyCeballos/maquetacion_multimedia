It's where your world meets. Discord is a voice, video, and text communication service used by over a hundred million people to chat and talk with their friends and communities.



Our Story




Discord is about empowering people to create spaces where they can find belonging in their lives. We aim to make it easy for you to regularly talk with the people you care about and to build genuine relationships with your friends and communities nearby or anywhere in the world. Original, reliable, playful, and relatable are the values that connect our users and employees.

Prologue

Discord was started to solve a big problem: how to communicate with friends around the world wild playing games online. since childhood, founders jason, citron and stan vishnevskiy both shared a love of video games, cherisshing the friendships and connections that formed while playing them. at the time, all the tools bulit for this job were slow, unrelieable, and complex, jason and stan, knew they could make a better service that encouranged talking, helped form memories, and recreated the feeling of togetherness all found through gaming.
